Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go! You can delete your Olitun account and all associated data using one of the methods below.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your learning progress, achievements, and personal data will be permanently removed.

In-App Deletion (Recommended)

The easiest way to delete your account is through the Olitun mobile app:

  1. Open the Olitun app on your device
  2. Go to Settings (tap the gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to the Account section
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Read the warning and confirm deletion

Your account will be deleted immediately and you'll be signed out.

Email Deletion Request

If you can't access the app, send an account deletion request via email:

  1. Send an email from your registered account email address
  2. Subject: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your registered email address in the message
  4. Send to: support@olitun.in
Processing Time: Email deletion requests are typically processed within 7 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.
Need Help?
If you have questions about account deletion or data privacy, contact us at support@olitun.in

What Gets Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials
  • All learning progress and achievements
  • App settings and preferences
  • Any stored personal information
